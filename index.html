02 / ABOUT

How I work

I sit between sales, engineering, and the customer. I turn discovery calls into demos that close, RFPs into wins, and product capability into the specific language a buyer needs to say yes.

Four years at Qmatic across government, healthcare, and enterprise. Deep experience with public sector procurement, ADA and WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, Power BI dashboard development, and custom demo environment configuration.

Before that: three years as a systems administrator at Purdue Federal Credit Union, automating endpoint provisioning and running internal training content.

Always: someone who believes the best technical content is the kind a non-technical buyer actually wants to watch.
