03 / THESIS

Why memory
archaeology?

The interesting thing about cultural memory isn't the big moments. It's the specific textures we forgot we shared. The taste of a Sbarro slice in a mall food court. The feel of a Blockbuster carpet. A gifted-and-talented pullout room that promised everything and delivered a worksheet.

These things disappear without ceremony. Nobody writes an obituary for the ritual of Friday night video store trips. That's the work. That's the channel.
